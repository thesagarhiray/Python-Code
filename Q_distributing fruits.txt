A teacher bought 100 apples, 95 mangoes and 90 Oranges from a farmer. 14.3% of the fruits could be damaged as per 
previous records. A teacher started distributing fruits in a classroom of 60 students. There are 40 boys and 20 
girls. Their seating arrangement is as follows. Write a program to print the following pattern. 

G1 G3
B1 B5
B2 B6
G2 G4
B3 B7
B4 B8

If the faculty starts distributing fruits in sequential order, write a program to calculate the following

1. quantity of apples, mangoes and oranges are allocated to girls.
2. quantity of damaged fruits allocated to total boys and total girls, respectively.
